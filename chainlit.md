# 🎬 Welcome to AI Movie Search!

## Intelligent Semantic Search

I'm your AI-powered movie recommendation assistant, using **Claude Sonnet 4.6** with a **1M token context window** to understand exactly what you're looking for.

---

## 🚀 How It Works

1. **Tell me what you want** - Use natural language
2. **I analyze your query** - Claude enhances it with semantic variations
3. **Search the database** - 1024-dim embeddings via Bedrock Titan v2
4. **Get intelligent results** - With AI-powered explanations
